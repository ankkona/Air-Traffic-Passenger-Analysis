# ✈️ Air Traffic Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an **Air Traffic Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze global air passenger traffic trends from **1995 to 2025**.

The dashboard helps users understand passenger movement, airline performance, terminal operations, regional distribution, and the impact of major events such as COVID-19.

## Objectives

* Analyze passenger traffic trends over time.
* Compare airline market performance.
* Identify the busiest terminals and boarding areas.
* Measure year-over-year passenger growth.
* Study passenger distribution across different periods.
* Provide interactive filtering by region and year.

## 🛠 Tools Used

- MS Excel
- Power Query
- Dax

### 2. Passenger Trend Analysis

A line chart shows passenger traffic growth from 1999–2025.

**Key Findings:**

* Passenger traffic steadily increased before 2020.
* A sharp decline occurred during the COVID-19 period.
* Passenger numbers recovered strongly after 2021.

---

### 3. Airline Market Analysis

A Pareto chart compares passenger volume across airlines.

**Insights:**

* United Airlines handled the highest passenger volume.
* A small group of airlines contributed to most passenger traffic.
* The cumulative percentage line highlights market concentration.

### 4. Domestic vs International Traffic

A donut chart compares passenger distribution.

**Results:**

* Domestic Passengers: **76.18%**
* International Passengers: **23.82%**

This indicates that domestic travel accounts for the majority of passenger traffic.

### 5. Passenger Distribution by Period

Passengers are grouped into:

* Pre-COVID
* COVID Impact
* Post-COVID

**Observations:**

* Passenger numbers were highest before COVID.
* Significant reduction occurred during the pandemic.
* Passenger traffic increased again in the post-COVID period.

### 6. Terminal & Boarding Area Operations

This visual shows passenger activity across terminals and boarding areas.

**Insights:**

* Terminal 3 recorded the highest traffic.
* Some boarding areas experienced significantly higher usage than others.

### 7. Year-over-Year Passenger Growth

A column chart displays annual growth percentages.

**Key Findings:**

* Strong growth in multiple years before 2020.
* Large negative growth during the COVID period.
* Rapid recovery growth after travel restrictions were lifted.

## 🎛 Interactive Features

Users can filter dashboard data using:

* **GEO Region Slicer**
* **Year Slicer**

This allows detailed analysis for specific regions and time periods.

* Domestic travel drives most passenger traffic.
